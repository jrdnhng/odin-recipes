Rice Recipe

Description

Learn how to make rice in just five easy steps. All this requires is rice, water and a rice cooker.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water

Steps

  1. Rinse the rice
  2. Put the rice into a rice cooker pot with a 1:1 ratio of water to rice
  3. Turn on the rice cooker by pressing the on button
  4. Wait until the rice cooker timer is up
  5. Enjoy
