Fried Egg Recipe

Description

Learn how to make a crispy fried egg with eggs, salt & pepper for seasoning, and oil.

Ingredients

  • Egg
  • Salt & pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Coat the frying pan in oil and pre-heat it on high heat.
  2. Once the pan is sizzling, crack an egg into the pan.
  3. Once the edges of the egg are brown and crispy, remove the egg from the pan.
  4. Add salt and pepper for seasoning.
  5. Enjoy
