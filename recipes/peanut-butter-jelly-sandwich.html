Peanut Butter & Jelly Sandwich Recipe

Description

No time to cook? This recipe will have you eating in less than two minutes.

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jam
  • Two slices of bread

Steps

  1. Slabber on the peanut butter on one slice of bread
  3. Slabber on jam on the other slice of bread
  4. Combine the two slices of bread with the peanut butter side facing the jam side.
  5. Enjoy
